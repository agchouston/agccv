Advocacy Month Recap

AGC Houston PAC Call Day

Once a year, AGC Houston hosts a PAC Call Day to raise funds for our political action committee. Those funds are disbursed to candidates and committees during the campaign season. On February 24th, 12 callers raised over $70,000 (now over $90,000) in pledges for our PAC/LAF. To learn more and contribute, please click here.

Leadership AGC Legislative Program

Each year, the Leadership AGC (LAGC) program hosts a session on the AGC-Texas Building Branch and legislative issues. During a year when the Texas Legislature is in session, LAGC participants would normally travel to the Texas Capitol in Austin and attend Legislative Day. This year, due to COVID-19 restrictions at the Capitol, we hosted a virtual AGC Legislative Briefing and Advocacy Program with the following presenters/presentations:

  • Jeremy Stovall, Brookstone, LP: Overview of AGC Houston Government Affairs Committee
  • Corbin Van Arsdale, AGC-Texas Building Branch: AGC-TBB History and the Texas Legislative Process
  • William Martinez, AGC Houston: Political Action Committee Overview
  • John Marshall, Satterfield & Pontikes Construction, Inc.: Laws and Sausages

Following the presentations, a Q&A session was hosted by Kenneth Loper, TAS Commercial Concrete, and Abby Bryant, Anslow Bryant Construction, Ltd. To learn more about the Leadership AGC Program, click here.

Meeting with Mayor Sylvester Turner

On March 15th, members of the AGC Houston Board and PAC contributors visited with Houston Mayor Sylvester Turner via Zoom to express appreciation for all he has done for the City and construction industry. Last year, during the initial coronavirus shutdowns, Mayor Turner and Judge Hidalgo deemed construction as an essential industry. To learn more, check out our video On Being Essential.

Legislative Week

From March 29th to April 6th, AGC Houston Legislative Week was held in conjunction with AGC-Texas Building Branch to advocate for industry priorities. Over the course of five meeting days, 20 members attended 16 scheduled Zoom meetings with offices from the Houston region.
